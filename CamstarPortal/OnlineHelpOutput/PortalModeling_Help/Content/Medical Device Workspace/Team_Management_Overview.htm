Team Management Overview

Note: The functionality described below is available only when Medical Device is configured. Refer to the Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics Medical Device User Guide or the Opcenter Execution Core Medical Device User Guide for information.

Team Management allows a team to be created and employees assigned to that team. The team can then be assigned to a work area eliminating the need for each employee to sign in separately. By default, a team can only be signed into one work area at a time. This can be overridden on the Team object. When the team is finished working at the work area, the team must be unassigned from the work area. Once a team is created, it remains intact but can be edited as needed.

The application verifies training requirements during assignment to the work area and also when the transaction is performed. If an individual employee is signing in to a work area, the employee's training records are validated. Training records for each team employee are validated when a team is assigned to a work area. If one team member does not have the proper validation, the assignment fails. When the transaction requiring team tracking is performed, the training records for all employees assigned to the work area, either from individual sign in or team assignment, are validated. If one employee does not have the proper training records, the transaction fails.
