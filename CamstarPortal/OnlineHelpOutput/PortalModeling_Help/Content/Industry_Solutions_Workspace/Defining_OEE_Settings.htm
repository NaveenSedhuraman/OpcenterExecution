Defining OEE Settings

The Real Time OEE Duration field on the OEE Settings modeling object enables you to set the time span for which the application calculates real time OEE. Options include:

• Day
• Shift
• Work

You can assign OEE Settings to these modeling objects:

• Resource - Refer to "When Assigning OEE Settings on the Resource" for information on assigning OEE Settings to a resource.
• Resource family - Refer to "When Assigning OEE Settings on the Resource Family" for information on assigning OEE Settings to a resource family.
• Factory - Refer to "Assigning OEE Settings on the Factory" for information on assigning OEE Settings on the factory.

When Defining OEE Settings

These rules apply when defining OEE Settings:

• The OEE Settings assigned on the Resource page take precedence over the OEE Settings assigned on the Resource Family page.
• The OEE Settings assigned on the Resource Family page take precedence over the OEE Settings assigned on the Factory page.

Refer to "When Assigning OEE Settings on the Resource" for information on assigning OEE Settings to a resource. Refer to "When Assigning OEE Settings on the Resource Family" for information on assigning OEE Settings to a resource family. Refer to the "Assigning OEE Settings on the Factory" for information on assigning OEE Settings to a factory.

How to Define OEE Settings

Follow these steps to define OEE Settings:

1. Open the OEE Settings page. The OEE Settings page appears within the Modeling page.
2. Click New. Blank fields appear for you to define a new instance.
3. Enter a name for the OEE Settings in the OEE Settings field.
4. Select a Real Time OEE Duration from the list.
5. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
6. Click Save. The application saves the modeling object and displays a success message.
