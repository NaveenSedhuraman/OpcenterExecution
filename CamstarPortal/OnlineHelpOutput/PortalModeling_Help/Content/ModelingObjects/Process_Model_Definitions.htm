Process Model Definitions

The process model is the control portion of the application's Information Model. It contains the objects that represent your manufacturing process flow: Container Level, Operation, Spec, Product Family, Product, Bill of Materials (BOM), ERP BOM, ERP Route, Recipe List, Master Recipe, and Workflow. Refer to "Defining Workflows" for information on the Workflow modeling object.
