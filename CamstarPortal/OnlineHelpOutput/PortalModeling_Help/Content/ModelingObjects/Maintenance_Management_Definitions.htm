Maintenance Management Introduction

The term maintenance refers to adjustments, calibrations, change of consumables, software upgrades, repairs, preventive maintenance, and similar activities carried out on designated resources (including carriers).

The Maintenance Management feature enables you to define and track resource-centric maintenance requirements based on calendar date and thruput. After maintenance requirements are assigned to resources, enforcement occurs during the execution of shop floor services.
