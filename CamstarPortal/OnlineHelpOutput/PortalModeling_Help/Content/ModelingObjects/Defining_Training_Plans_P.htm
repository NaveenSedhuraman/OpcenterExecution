Defining Training Plans

A Training Plan consists of one or more training requirements and sub-training plans. Trainers and managers jointly determine and create the appropriate training plan for an employee, assign the plan to the employee, and then modify the plan as needed. Plans are modified when new requirements and new sub-training plans are added to the training plan. Training Plan is referenced by Employee.

Note: Those responsible for administering training plans will most likely not remove the training requirements that are added to the plan, simply because these requirements referenced by the plan are bound by effectivity dates that will resolve when the user is trying to perform an action that requires training.

When Defining Training Plans

Training plan contains the optional modeling definitions:

• Training plan, as a sub-plan
• Training requirement

The application requires you to specify these objects to save the training plan when you add a corresponding training detail to the Training Plan Detail grid. For example, if you add a training requirement row to the Training Plan Detail grid, you must associate an existing training requirement before the application will allow you to save the training plan.

Your ability to edit column values in the Training Plan Detail grid depends on the row's training detail type. The Training Plan column is only enabled for sub-training plan details while the Training Requirement column is only enabled for training requirement details.

Note: You can have multiple details that combine training requirements and sub-training plans.

How to Define a Training Plan

Follow these steps to define a Training Plan:

1. Open the Training Plan page. The Training Plan page appears within the Modeling page.
2. Click New. Blank fields appear for you to define a new instance.
3. Enter a name in the Training Plan field.
4. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
5. Click Save. The application displays a success message stating that the Training Plan has been added.

How to Add a Sub-Training Plan

Follow these steps to add a Sub-Training Plan to a Training Plan:

1. Perform the "How to Define a Training Plan" procedure.
2. Select Sub-Training Plan from the Training Detail Type field.
3. Click in a blank row in the Training Detail Type grid.

Or

Click Add new row in the Training Plan Detail grid. A new row appears.

4. Select a Training Plan from the Training Plan menu in the Training Plan Detail grid.
5. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
6. Click outside of the grid to add the Training Plan.
7. Click Save. The application displays a success message indicating the modeling object was updated.

How to Add a Training Requirement

Follow these steps to add a Training Requirement to a Training Plan:

1. Perform the "How to Define a Training Plan" procedure.
2. Select Training Requirement from the Training Detail Type field.
3. Click in a blank row in the Training Detail Type grid.

Or

Click Add new row in the Training Plan Detail grid. A new row appears.

4. Select a Training Requirement or its revision from the Training Requirement menu in the Training Plan Detail grid.
5. Select the Is Revision of Record check box if the revision is the ROR.
6. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
7. Click outside of the grid to add the Training Plan.
8. Click Save. The application displays a success message indicating the modeling object was updated.
