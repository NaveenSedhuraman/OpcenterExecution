Defining Carrier Status Reasons

A Carrier Status Reason provides an additional level of detail to describe the reason for the current status of the carrier. For example, Sterilization Process Failed could describe the reason for a carrier status of Contaminated. The standardized reason codes can be assigned to carriers, and help your operators make more informed decisions on the shop floor.

Refer to "Common Fields on Modeling Pages" for information on the fields common to all modeling objects.

How to Define a Carrier Status Reason

Follow these steps to define a Carrier Status Reason:

1. Open the Carrier Status Reason page. The Carrier Status Reason page appears within the Modeling page.
2. Click New. Blank fields appear for you to define a new instance.
3. Enter a name for the status reason in the Carrier Status Reason field.
4. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to "Common Fields on Modeling Pages" for information on the optional fields.
5. Click Save. The application saves the modeling object and displays a success message.
