Defining Customers

A Customer is an entity to which goods and services are provided. Typically, a customer is another company but can also be another factory or department within the enterprise. Product definitions can designate a specific customer for whom the product is produced.

When Defining a Customer

Customer is an optional field in the definition of Product and Shipment Destination and in the transactions Start and Container Maintenance.

Note: A Customer definition can have related WIP Messages if it is associated with a field from a container definition. Refer to "Defining WIP Messages" and the Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics Shop Floor User Guide or the Opcenter Execution Core Shop Floor User Guide for information.

How to Define a Customer

Follow these steps to define a Customer:

1. Open the Customer page. The Customer page appears within the Modeling page.
2. Click New. Blank fields appear for you to define a new instance.
3. Enter a customer name in the Customer field.
4. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
5. Click Save. The application saves the modeling object and displays a success message.

How to Add Customer Contacts

Follow these steps to add a contact to a customer:

1. Perform the "How to Define a Customer" procedure

Or

Select an existing Customer instance.

2. Expand the Contacts section.
3. Click Add new row on the Customer Contacts grid. The Contact Detail pop-up appears.
4. Enter the full name of the customer contact in the Full Name field.
5. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
6. Click OK. The contact appears in the Customer Contacts grid.
7. Repeat steps 3-6 to add additional contacts.
8. Click Save. The application displays a success message indicating the modeling object was updated.
