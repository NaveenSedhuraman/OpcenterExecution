Change Management Definitions

Change Management is a manufacturing process change management application to help manufacturers manage the lifecycle of changes to their product and process definitions. For example, an organization might export modeling object instances from their test system and import those instances on their production system.

You define change management business processes in your information model to use this application for your change management transactions. Refer to the Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics Change Management User Guide or the Opcenter Execution Core Change Management User Guide for information on performing change management transactions.
