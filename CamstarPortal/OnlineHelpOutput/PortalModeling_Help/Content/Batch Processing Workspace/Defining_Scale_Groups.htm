Defining Scale Groups

A Scale Group is a subclass of Resource Group that represents a group of scales and can include references to individual scales or to other scale groups. Grouping scales allows modeling objects to reference multiple, but related, scales as a collection rather than referring to one scale at a time. You can select scale groups on which to schedule maintenance.

You can assign scale groups to specs, products, product families, or weigh issue tasks to define the scales allowed to weigh components.The scale group assigned to the weigh issue task takes precedence over all other assigned scale groups if a scale group is assigned to more than one of these objects. The scale group assigned to the product takes precedence over product families and specs. The scale group assigned to the product family takes precedence over the spec. If no scale group is assigned to any of these objects, you can select any available scale for weighing.

Refer to "Assigning a Scale Group on the Spec" for information on assigning a scale group on the spec. Refer to "Assigning a Scale Group to a Component Product" for information on assigning a scale group on the product. Refer to "Assigning a Scale Group on the Product Family" for information on assigning a scale group on the product family. Refer to "When Defining Weigh Issue Tasks" for information on assigning a scale group on the weigh issue task.

How to Define a Scale Group

Follow these steps to define a Scale Group:

1. Open the Scale Group page. The Scale Group page appears within the Modeling page.
2. Click New. Blank fields appear for you to define a new instance.
3. Enter a name for this scale group in the Scale Group field.
4. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
5. Click Save. The application saves the modeling object and displays a success message.
