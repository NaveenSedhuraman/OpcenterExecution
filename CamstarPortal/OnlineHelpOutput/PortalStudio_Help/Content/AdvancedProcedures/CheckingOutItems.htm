Checking Items In and Out

Portal Studio allows you to check pages and web parts out of and back in to your source control system. Checked out pages and web parts are indicated by a red arrow on the item's icon in the UI Components catalog.

When Checking Items In and Out

When you check out a work item, other users can see the item, but they cannot make or save any changes to the item. Other Portal Studio users will see a padlock on a checked-out work item.

Items are checked out at the individual item level. An item checked out in one workspace is checked out of all other workspaces. Refer to "Developing in Workspaces" for information.

Once you check in an item, it is available for all users to check out.

How to Check Items In and Out

Follow these steps to check items in and out:

1. Access Portal Studio.
2. Open a work item.
3. Click Check Out. The item is checked out of your source control system.
4. Perform any necessary work.
5. Click Save. The application saves your changes and displays a success message.
6. Click Check In. The item is checked into your source control system.
