Configuring Localization Settings

You can configure the date, time, and decimal settings for Portal pages in Portal Studio.

When Configuring Localization Settings

You can configure date, time, and decimal settings through your internet browser's preferred language settings or through Portal Studio's settings.

Setting a preferred language in your internet browser formats Portal pages so that date, time, and decimal values are in the preferred language's format. This setting only affects your instance of the Portal. Refer to your internet browser's help for information on how to set a preferred language.

Portal Studio's current culture settings control the date, time, and decimal settings on Portal pages. Current culture settings are customizable and override your browser settings.

Important: Changing the current culture settings in Portal Studio causes a global change and overrides any individual browser based settings for all Portal users.

The accepted values for current culture settings are based on Microsoft's values for custom date and time format strings.

How to Configure Localizaton Settings

Follow these steps to configure localization settings:

1. Access Portal Studio.
2. Click the Portal Settings button. The Portal Settings pop-up appears.
3. Select CurrentCultureSettings from the list of settings. The properties for CurrentCultureSettings appear.
4. Expand DateTimeFormat.
5. Enter values in the LongTimePattern and ShortDatePattern fields.
6. Expand NumberFormat.
7. Enter a value in the NumberDecimalSeparator field.
8. Click OK. The Portal Settings pop-up closes.
