Maintenance Management

After a resource's maintenance requirements (Thruput, Date, and Recurring Date) have been met, you use the Maintenance Management transaction to mark the required maintenance for the resource as complete. The maintenance must be marked as completed before the resource can be used for further processing.

A system administrator can configure the maintenance requirements to send e-mail alerts at specified periods before, on, and after the due date to ensure maintenance is completed and the resource remains available for processing. The e-mail messages are not generated automatically when maintenance becomes due, but when the operator completes a transaction on the resource, such as Move In or Resource Thruput.
