Managing Inventory

The Manage Inventory page enables you to do the following:

• Assign containers, lots, or products to an inventory location
• Update product or quantity information for a lot
• Update quantity information for a product
• Select a removal strategy for a product
• Transfer a container, lot, or product to another inventory location
• Remove a container, lot, or product from an inventory location

Refer to the Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics Modeling User Guide or the Opcenter Execution Core Modeling User Guide for information on defining inventory locations.

How to Assign a Container, Lot, or Product to an Inventory Location

Follow these steps to assign a container, lot, or product to an inventory location:

1. Open the Manage Inventory page. The Manage Inventory page appears.
2. Select an Inventory Location from the list.
3. Do you want to assign a container, lot, or product to an inventory location?

If you want to assign a . . .

Then . . .

Container
a. Select a Container from the list. The application disables the Lot field. The application displays the container's product, current quantity, and UOM. The Submit button appears.
b. Go to step 4.

Lot
a. Enter a lot in the Lot field. The application populates the Product field with a list of available products. The application makes the Qty field writable. The application populates the UOM field with a list of available units of measure. The Submit button appears.
b. Select a Product from the list. The Submit button appears.
c. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
d. Go to step 4.

Product
a. Select a Product from the list. The Submit button appears.
b. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
c. Go to step 4.
4. Click Submit. The application adds the container, lot, or product information to the Inventory Details grid. The application also adds the information to the Inventory grid of the Inventory Location modeling instance.

How to Update Product or Quantity Information for a Lot

Follow these steps to update product or quantity information for a lot:

1. Open the Manage Inventory page. The Manage Inventory page appears.
2. Select an Inventory Location from the list.
3. Enter the name of the lot in the Lot field. The Submit button appears.
4. Do you want to update the product or the quantity?

If . . .

Then . . .

Product
a. Select a Product from the list.
b. Go to step 5.

Quantity
a. Enter a lot in the Lot field. The application populates the Product field with a list of available products. The application makes the Qty field writable. The application populates the UOM field with a list of available units of measure. The Submit button appears.
b. Select a Product from the list.
c. Enter the quantity in the Qty field.
d. Go to step 5.
5. Click Submit. The application updates the container or lot information in the Inventory Details grid. The application also updates the information to the Inventory grid of Inventory Location modeling instance.

How to Transfer a Container, Lot, or Product to Another Inventory Location

Follow these steps to transfer a container, lot, or product to another inventory location:

1. Open the Manage Inventory page. The Manage Inventory page appears.
2. Select an Inventory Location field from the list. The application populates the Inventory Details grid.
3. Select the check box of a container, lot, or product in the Inventory Details grid. The To Inventory Location field, the Transfer button, and the Remove button appear.
4. Select a new inventory location in the To Inventory Location field.
5. Click Transfer. The application removes the container, lot, or product from the Inventory Details grid. The application also transfers the container, lot, or product to the new Inventory Location modeling instance.

How to Remove a Container, Lot, or Product from an Inventory Location

Follow these steps to remove a container, lot, or product from an inventory location:

1. Open the Manage Inventory page. The Manage Inventory page appears.
2. Select an Inventory Location from the list. The application populates the Inventory Details grid.
3. Select the check box of a, container, lot, or product in the Inventory Details grid. The To Inventory Location field, the Transfer button, and the Remove button appear.
4. Click Remove. The application removes the container, lot, or product from the Inventory Details grid.
