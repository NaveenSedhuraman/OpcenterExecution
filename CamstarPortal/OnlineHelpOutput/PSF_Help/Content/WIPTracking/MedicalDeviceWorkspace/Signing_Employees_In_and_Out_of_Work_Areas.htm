Signing Employees In and Out of Work Areas

You can use the Work Area Sign In Sign Out page to sign employees in and out of work areas.

When Signing In to a Work Area

The application displays a list of available work areas based on the selection made in the Sign In Type field. The name of the list changes dynamically when you select the Sign In Type. You must select an instance from the list to sign in to that work area.

When Signing In to Multiple Work Areas

You must sign in to each area individually when signing in to multiple work areas.

When Signing Out of Multiple Work Areas

You can select the check box in the header or select multiple items in the list to sign out of multiple work areas at the same time.

How to Sign In to a Work Area

Follow these steps to sign in to a work area:

1. Open the Work Area Sign In Sign Out page. The Work Area Sign In Sign Out page appears.
2. Select an Employee from the list.
3. Select a work area in the Sign In Type field. The application displays a field containing a list of instances available for the selected work area.
4. Select an instance.
5. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
6. Click Sign In. The application displays a message that the employee is signed in to the work area.

How to Sign Out of a Work Area

Follow these steps to sign out of a work area:

1. Open the Work Area Sign In Sign Out page. The Work Area Sign In Sign Out page appears.
2. Select an Employee from the list. The application populates the Signed In Work Areas grid with work areas where the employee is signed in.
