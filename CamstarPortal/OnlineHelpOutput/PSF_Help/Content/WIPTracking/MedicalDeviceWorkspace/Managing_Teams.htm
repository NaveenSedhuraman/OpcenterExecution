Managing Teams

Team Management functionality enables you to use the Manage Team Employees page to add employees to or remove employees from teams. You can assign an employee to more than one team if the Allow Multiple Teams check box is selected on the Employee page. Refer to the Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics Modeling User Guide or the Opcenter Execution Core Modeling User Guide for information on creating teams.

You can also use the Manage Team Employees page to sign teams in or out of work areas.

When Signing Teams In to a Work Area

You must select a team and click the Assign Team button to display the Work Area Sign In Sign Out pop-up. The application displays a list of available work areas based on the selection made in the Sign In Type field. The name of the list changes dynamically when you select the Sign In Type. You must select an instance from the list to sign a team in to that work area.

How to Add Employees to a Team

Follow these steps to add employees to a team:

1. Open the Manage Team Employees page. The Manage Team Employees page appears.
2. Select a Team from the list. The application populates the Addable Employees and the Team Employees grids. The application also enables the Add Employees, Remove Employees, and Assign Team buttons.
3. Select the check box for one or more employees you want to add to the team.
4. Click Add Employees. The application removes the name of the selected employee(s) from the Addable Employees grid and moves it to the Team Employees grid.

How to Remove Employees from a Team

Follow these steps to remove employees from a team:

1. Open the Manage Team Employees page. The Manage Team Employees page appears.
2. Select a Team from the list. The application populates the Addable Employees and the Team Employees grids. The application also enables the Add Employees, Remove Employees, and Assign Team buttons.
3. Select the check box for one or more employees you want to remove from the team.
4. Click Remove Employees. The application removes the name of the selected employee(s) from the Team Employees grid and moves it to the Addable Employees grid.

How to Sign a Team In to a Work Area

Follow these steps to sign a team in to a work area:

1. Open the Manage Team Employees page. The Manage Team Employees page appears.
2. Select a Team from the list. The application populates the Addable Employees and the Team Employees grids. The application also enables the Add Employees, Remove Employees, and Assign Team buttons.
3. Click Assign Team. The Work Area Sign In Sign Out pop-up appears with the Team name populated.
4. Select a work area in the Sign in Type field. The application displays a field containing a list of instances available for the selected work area.
5. Select an instance.
6. Enter optional information according to your business requirements. Refer to the field definitions table for information on the optional fields.
7. Click Sign In. The Work Area Sign In Sign Out pop-up closes. The application displays a message that the team is successfully signed in to the work area. The work area appears in the Sign In Work Areas grids

How to Sign a Team Out of One or More Work Areas

Follow these steps to sign a team out of one or more work areas:

1. Open the Manage Team Employees page. The Manage Team Employees page appears.
2. Select a Team from the list. The application populates the Addable Employees and the Team Employees grids. The application also enables the Add Employees, Remove Employees, and Assign Team buttons.
3. Click Assign Team. The Work Area Sign In Sign Out pop-up appears with the Team name and the Signed in Work Areas grid populated.
4. Select the check box of one or more work areas in the Signed in Work Areas grid.
5. Click Sign Out. The Work Area Sign In Sign Out pop-up closes. The application displays a message that the team is successfully signed out of the work areas. The application removes the work areas from the Sign In Work Areas grid.
