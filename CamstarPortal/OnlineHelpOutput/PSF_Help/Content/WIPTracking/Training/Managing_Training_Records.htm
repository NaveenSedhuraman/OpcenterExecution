Managing Training Records

The separately licensed Operator Training and Certification module enables employers to assign training requirements to an employee and track an employee's training record status for those training requirements.

Training records administration can only be performed by trainers as designated in the training requirement definitions. Training records administration enables you to add, update, and delete training records. It also enables you to compare two employee's training requirements, and copy training records from one employee to another.

These topics provide information on training records management:
