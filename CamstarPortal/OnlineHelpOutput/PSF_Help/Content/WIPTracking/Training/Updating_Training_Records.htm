Updating Training Records

You can update training records for an employee using the Update Record button available on the Training Record Management page. Update a training record when you need to change the training requirement, status, or expiration date associated with the record.

When Updating Training Records

You must search for the record you want to update and then select it in the Training Records grid.

Only training requirements for which you are identified as the trainer are included in the Training Requirement list. You can select a particular revision of the training requirement.

Training Record Pop-up

The Training Record pop-up appears when you select a record and then click Update Record on the Training Record Management page.

How to Update a Training Record

Follow these steps to update a Training Record:

1. Open the Training Record Management page from the Training menu. The Training Record Management page appears.
2. Search for the record you want to update using one or more of these search criteria for retrieving training record information:
• Enter or select an Employee.
• Enter or select a Training Requirement.
• Enter or select a Status.
3. Click Search. The Training Records grid is populated with the records matching your criteria.
4. Select the record you want to update in the Training Records grid.
5. Click Update Record. The Training Record pop-up appears.
6. Enter or select a training requirement in the Training Requirement field and select a revision, if necessary.
7. Enter or select the record's status in the Status field.
8. Enter or select an electronic signature requirement in the ESig Requirement field, if applicable.
9. Enter or select a date in the Expiration Date field, if necessary.
10. Click Submit. The Training Record pop-up closes and the updated record appears in the Training Records grid on the Training Record Management page.
