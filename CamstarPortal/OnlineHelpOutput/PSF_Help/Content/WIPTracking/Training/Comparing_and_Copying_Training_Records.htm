Comparing and Copying Training Records

The Training Records Comparison transaction facilitates training records creation by comparing two employees' training records and copying a record from one to the other. This is useful when you have a new employee that needs to undergo the same or similar training regimen as another employee whose records are already defined. By copying an existing record, you can then modify it according to your requirements instead of creating the record from the beginning.

When Copying Employee Records

Use the Copy Record button to copy the record selected in the From Employee's training record grid to the To Employee's training record grid. The application enables this button when you select an employee in the From Employee field and an employee in the To Employee field.

You do not have to copy all the training records to an employee at one time or from one particular employee. You can copy one or more training records from one employee, and then copy additional records from another employee at a later date.

For example, you can copy a basic safety course and assembly operation training requirements from Employee A to Employee B. Later, if you decide to add training for other operations, you can copy them from Employee C to Employee B.

You can use one employee’s training records to update several other employee’s training records. Leave the same employee in the From Employee field and continue to select new employees in the To Employee field. Remember to click Copy Record and save the record before selecting a new To Employee.

When Viewing the Employee Training Record Grids

The grids displaying the training records for the specified To and From employees are display only. Training requirements and the training requirement statuses are defined in Modeling. Refer to theOpcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics Modeling User Guide or Opcenter Execution Core Modeling User Guide for information.

Note: An expiration date appears for a training requirement only if a date was specified when the training requirement was defined.

How to Compare Employee Training Records

Follow these steps to compare employee training records:

1. Open the Training Record Comparison page from the Training menu. The Training Record Comparison page appears.
2. Select an employee in the From Employee field. The training records assigned to this employee appear in the grid below the From Employee field.
3. Select an employee in the To Employee field. The training records assigned to this employee appear in the grid below the To Employee field and the application enables the Copy Record button.
4. Compare the training records displayed in the two grids.

How to Copy Training Records to Another Employee

Follow the steps below to copy a training record from one employee to another:

1. Open the Training Record Comparison page from the Training menu. The Training Record Comparison page appears.
2. Select the employee whose training records you want to copy in the From Employee field. The training records assigned to this employee appear in the grid below the From Employee field.
3. Select the employee for whom you want to copy the training records in the To Employee field. The training records assigned to this employee appear in the grid below the To Employee field and the application enables the Copy Record button.
4. Select the training record you want to copy in the From Employee's training record grid.
5. Click Copy Record. A copy of the record associated with the employee is added to the To Employee's training record grid.
6. Repeat steps 2-5 to copy additional records.
7. Click Save. The application displays a status message confirming the copied records were added for the employee.
