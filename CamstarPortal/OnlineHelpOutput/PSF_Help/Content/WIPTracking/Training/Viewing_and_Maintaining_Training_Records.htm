Viewing and Maintaining Training Records

You can associate employees with one or more training records if you are a designated trainer. These records enable trainers to track an employee's status against a particular training requirement. Each training record provides:

• Training record status
• Training requirement

When Searching for Training Records

You can specify a single criteria or a combination of search criteria when searching for training records using the Search button on the Training Record Management page. For example, you can use the Training Requirement field to search for a particular employee's training records, or for all employees who are assigned to a basic health and safety training record.

You can further refine your search by using the Status field in addition to the Training Requirement field to limit your search to only those employees who have not completed a particular requirement such as a basic safety course.

Training Record Management Page

Use the Training Record Management page to:

• Retrieve training records based on certain criteria.
• Add training records for an employee.
• View, update, and delete training records.

The top part of the page has a display-only field that shows the trainer who accessed the training record management menu. The Training Records grid displays a list of existing records based on the selection criteria values you enter.

Selection criteria enables you to search the training records for:

• A specific employee
• A specific type of training such as Basic Component Assembly
• Training records by status (such as complete or incomplete)

How to View Training Records

Follow these steps to view training records:

1. Open the Training Record Management page from the Training menu. The Training Record Management page appears.
2. Do you want to view all training records?

If . . .

Then . . .

Yes
a. Leave the selection criteria fields blank
b. Go to step 3.

No
a. Enter or select one or more of these search criteria for retrieving training record information:
• Employee
• Training Requirement
• Status
b. Go to step 3.
3. Click Search. The Training Records grid is populated with the records matching your criteria.

How to Delete a Training Record

Follow these steps to delete a Training Record:

1. Open the Training Record Management page from the Training menu. The Training Record Management page appears.
2. Enter or select the record you want to delete using one or more of these search criteria for retrieving training record information:
• Employee
• Training Requirement
• Status
3. Click Search. The Training Records grid is populated with the records matching your criteria.
4. Select the record you want to delete from the Training Records grid.
5. Click Delete Record. The record is removed from the grid.
6. Repeat steps 4-5 to delete additional records.
