Adding Training Records

You can add training records for an employee using the Add Record button available on the Training Record Management page.

You add training records when you want to ensure an employee is meeting a particular training plan. For example, all employees may need to take a basic safety course while an assembler may also have to train on a particular assembly operation.

When Adding Training Records

You must define the following in your Factory model before your training records can be added:

• Training Requirements
• Training Record Status Codes
• Employees

You must be a designated trainer to add training records for an employee. No training requirements are available to select if you are not the designated trainer.

When selecting a training requirement, you can expand the requirement to select a specific revision of that requirement if you want a revision other than the revision of record.

The record always references a specific revision. When you select the revision of record and add the record, the application picks up the latest revision of record if you update the training requirement in the future.

Add Training Record Pop-up

The Add Training Record pop-up appears when you select Add Record on the Training Record page.

How to Add a Training Record

Follow these steps to add a training record:

1. Open the Training Record Management page from the Training menu. The Training Record Management page appears.
2. Click Add Record. The Add Training Record pop-up appears.
3. Enter or select the employee's name in the Employee field.
4. Enter or select a training requirement in the Training Requirement field and select a revision, if necessary.
5. Enter or select the record's status in the Status field.
6. Enter or select an electronic signature requirement in the ESig Requirement field, if applicable.
7. Click Submit. The Add Training Record pop-up closes and the new record appears in the Training Records grid on the Training Record Management page.
