Performing Material Acknowledgment

The Material Request Acknowledge page enables you to acknowledge or cancel open material replenishment requests. Acknowledgments indicate that material has been received. Cancellations indicate that the material request is canceled.

When Performing Material Acknowledgment

The application updates the material quantities on the associated material queue or inventory location when you acknowledge that material has been received. The row is removed from the Material Replenishment Request grid if the Remaining Quantity reaches zero.

The acknowledged material will be loaded as a new row on the material queue or inventory location if the product:

• Has a unique Container/Lot identification and does not already exist for the Product loaded in the Material Queue or Inventory Location
• Doesn’t have a Container/Lot number, but the same Product(s) in the Material Queue or Inventory Location have Container/Lot numbers

How to Acknowledge a Material Request

Follow these steps to acknowledge a material request:

1. Open the Material Request Acknowledge page.
2. Select an Inventory Location or Material Queue. The application populates the grid based on your selection.
3. Select the grid row listing the inventory to acknowledge.
4. Enter the Received Qty amount. The application populates Qty Remaining with the difference between Qty Requested and Qty Received.
5. Perform one of the following:

If the received material . . .

Then . . .

Has a container/lot identifier
a. Scan or enter the identifier into the Container/Lot field.
b. Go step 6.

Does not have a container/lot identifier
a. Go to step 6.
6. Repeat steps 3-5 for each additional material request in the grid that you need to acknowledge.
7. Click Submit. A message is sent to the Valor Material Management System with the acknowledgment or acknowledgments, if configured. The Received Qty of material is loaded on to the applicable inventory location or material queue. The Remaining Qty field is updated with the difference between the Requested Qty and Received Qty.

How to Cancel a Material Request

Follow these steps to cancel a material request:

1. Open the Material Request Acknowledge page.
2. Select an Inventory Location or Material Queue. The application populates the grid based on your selection if you changed the default.
3. Select the grid row listing the material request to cancel.
4. Select the Cancel check box.
5. Repeat steps 3-4 for each additional material request in the grid that you need to cancel.
6. Click Submit. The application cancels the requests and removes the requests from the Material Request Status Board. A message is sent to Valor Material Management System with the cancellation or cancellations, if configured.
