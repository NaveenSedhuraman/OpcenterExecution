Industry Solutions Workspace

Note: The functionality described below is available only when the Industry Solutions workspace is installed. Refer to the Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics Industry Solutions User Guide or the Opcenter Execution Core Industry Solutions User Guide for information.

The Industry Solutions workspace provides a mechanism for delivering features separately from the Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics or Opcenter Execution Core. Industry Solutions features are developed in the Industry Solutions workspace and are therefore isolated. This enables the industry solutions-specific functionality to remain separate while still allowing the inheritance of subsequent Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics or Opcenter Execution Core changes.

The Industry Solutions workspace contains functionality associated with:

• Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)
• Resource Material Queue (RMQ)

These topics provide information on the Industry Solutions workspace:

• Overall Equipment Effectiveness Overview
• Resource Material Queue Overview
• Viewing OEE Data
• Viewing OEE Details
• Managing Inventory
• Managing Material Queues
