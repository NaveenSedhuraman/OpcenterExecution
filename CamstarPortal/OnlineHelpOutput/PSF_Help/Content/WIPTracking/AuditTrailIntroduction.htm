Audit Trail

The manufacturing audit trail provides a complete history of all transactions for a container, carrier, or an event. All data in the container history files is available for display, such as materials consumed, processes used, parametric data collected, exceptions and deviations, dates and times, and electronic signatures. The resource audit trail provides a complete history of all transactions for a resource. All data in the resource history files is available for display such as the machine where material is processed, parametric data collected, and dates and times.
