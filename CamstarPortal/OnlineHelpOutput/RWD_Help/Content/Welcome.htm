About Siemens Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics & Siemens Opcenter Execution Core

Siemens Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics and Siemens Opcenter Execution Core are the foundation for top-tier global manufacturers and product innovators to enable rapid change, lean manufacturing, consistent quality output, rapid NPI, and higher profit margins. It provides an easy-to-use manufacturing execution system (MES) with quality monitoring and enforcement, instant visibility and intelligence, and easy interoperability with business systems and shop floor automation. Solutions are architected for global use, enabling worldwide visibility for real-time control and mission-critical operations.

Features

• Automatic enforcement of manufacturing processes
• Self-auditing, paperless manufacturing
• Global product, production, process dashboards
• Fast, accurate process change management
• Standards-based, robust interoperability with any application

Benefits

• Reduced variability
• Realize granular enterprise visibility
• Achieve continuous improvement
• Reduce brand risk
• Improve profit margins
• Complete manufacturing audit trails across the enterprise
• Continuous feedback to operators
• Instant access to production, process and quality dashboards
