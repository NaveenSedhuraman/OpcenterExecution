Removing Local Portal Studio Shortcuts

Once Portal Studio shortcuts have been installed on your local environment, they can also be removed from your local environment.

When Removing Portal Studio Shortcuts

When you select Remove this application from the shortcut menu, you are only removing the menu or desktop shortcut to Portal Studio. Portal Studio will still be available from the Camstar Portal after removing the local shortcuts.

When you select Remove this application, a dialog box appears to confirm you want to remove the shortcuts.

How to Remove Portal Studio Shortcuts

Follow these steps to remove Portal Studio shortcuts:

1. Access Portal Studio through the Portal or through your local installation.
2. Right-click in the work area. A shortcut menu appears.
3. Select Remove this application. A warning message appears verifying you want to remove the shortcuts.
4. Click Yes to remove the shortcuts.
