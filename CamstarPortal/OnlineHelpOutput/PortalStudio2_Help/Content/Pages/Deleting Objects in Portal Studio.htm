Deleting Components

You can permanently delete components in Portal Studio.

Note: Components deleted in Portal Studio are permanently removed from the application and cannot be recovered.

How to Delete Components

Follow these steps to delete components:

1. Access Portal Studio.
2. Open a component from the components catalog. The component appears in the working area.
3. Click Delete on the Portal Studio global toolbar. The Delete <Component> confirmation message appears.
4. Click OK. The Delete <Component> confirmation closes and the component is deleted.
5. Click Save. The application saves your changes and displays a success message.
