Managing Records

Opcenter Medical Device and Diagnostics (Opcenter EX MDD) and Opcenter Execution Core (Opcenter EX CR) provide the ability to manage quality records (events and nonconformances) once they have been recorded. Features include these abilities:

• Searching for a quality record
• Viewing a record
• Performing actions on the record such as changing categories, reassigning the owner, and so on
• Editing a record

Your ability to view or edit a record and to perform actions on it depend on whether you are the record owner or another user with the appropriate permissions.
