Opcenter Execution Core Intelligence

Opcenter Execution Core Intelligence (Opcenter EX CR Intelligence) uses powerful software from SAP® BusinessObjects™ to bring real-time business intelligence (BI) information to your enterprise. Opcenter EX CR Intelligence is a separately licensed module. By using Opcenter EX CR Intelligence, you can quickly assess the efficiency and quality of a factory’s production process. Opcenter EX CR Intelligence is a combination of SAP® BusinessObjects™ products and components of the Opcenter EX CR and Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics (Opcenter EX MDD) applications.

Opcenter EX CR Intelligence supports Microsoft SQL Server and Oracle database management systems. The Portal-based interface gives you access to multiple reports and dashboards, ensuring you can react quickly to changes in your business environment.

Reports

Opcenter EX CR Intelligence includes SAP® BusinessObjects™ Web Intelligence reports and SAP® Crystal Reports. These reports are used to monitor specific aspects of your production environment. Refer to the Reports chapter in the Opcenter EX CR Intelligence Reference for information on the available reports.

Dashboards

An Opcenter EX MDD or Opcenter EX CR dashboard is a visual representation of four aspects of a factory’s production in one place. After a quick analysis of a dashboard, you can target specific areas of your production for improvement. The application includes three default dashboards, including one for managing quality. The default Quality Management dashboard allows you to view various aspects of quality data associated with a factory. Refer to the Opcenter EX CR Intelligence Reference for information on the available dashboards.

Performance Indicators

Performance indicators offer a way to monitor critical aspects of your manufacturing processes. Performance indicators also inform you of the appropriate actions required to maintain optimal operational efficiency. Refer to the Performance Indicators chapter in the Opcenter EX CR Intelligence Reference for information on the available performance indicators.
