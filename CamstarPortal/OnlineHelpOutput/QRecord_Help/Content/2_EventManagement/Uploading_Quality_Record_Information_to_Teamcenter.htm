Uploading Quality Record Information to Teamcenter

You can initiate the creation of an event in Teamcenter from an event in CEP when your organization has integrated Teamcenter with CEP.

Use the Upload to PLM button to send quality record information to Teamcenter. A problem report is created in Teamcenter after the information is sent successfully and an Upload Event to PLM transaction is recorded in the Manufacturing Audit Trail.

Refer to the Opcenter EX CR Teamcenter Integration Guide for information.
