Approving a Quality Record Resolution

Use the Approve action to approve a quality record resolution.

You can approve a quality record resolution when:

• The status of the record is In Review.
• You are a specified approver.

The application disables the Approval button when either of the above conditions are not met.

Note: After all approvals for the quality record resolution have been completed, you can view the current approvals from the record’s Quality Object Resolution page or the Resolution tab of the record’s management page.

Refer to "Routing and Capturing Approvals" for information on performing approvals.
