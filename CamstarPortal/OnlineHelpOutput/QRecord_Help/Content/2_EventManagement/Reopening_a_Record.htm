Reopening a Record

Use the Reopen action to open a closed event record. The application enables the Reopen action for an event record only if the status of the record is Closed.

When Reopening an Event Record

When you reopen an event record, the application sets the status of the record to Active, closes the Reopen page, and displays the record’s management page. The application closes the Reopen page, displays the record’s management page, and does not change the status of the record if you click Cancel while on the Reopen page.

You can reopen a closed record when:

• The status of the record is Closed.
• You have permission to reopen a record.

The application disables the Reopen button when either of the above conditions are not met.

How to Reopen a Record

Follow these steps to reopen a record:

1. Access the quality record. The record's management page appears.
2. Click Reopen in the Quality Actions pane. The Reopen page appears.
3. Enter Comments explaining why you need to reopen the record.
4. Click Submit. The application displays a message indicating the record was opened and displays the record’s management page.
