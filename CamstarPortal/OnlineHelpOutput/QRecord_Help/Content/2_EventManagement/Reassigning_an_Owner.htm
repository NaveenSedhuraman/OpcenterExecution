Reassigning an Owner

Use the Reassign action to assign a new owner to the record. When you assign a new owner to a record, the application adds a task to the new owner’s Concierge and Message Center and removes the task from the Concierge and Message Center of the previous owner. The application automatically generates an e-mail for the new and previous owners notifying them of the change in assignment, if a system administrator has configured an owner reassignment e-mail message.

When Reassigning an Owner

When reassigning an owner, the roles available for selection depend on the organization associated with the event. The selected role then determines the owners available for selection.

Any information entered in the Comments field will be available as part of the record’s history. Comments entered here will not appear on the record’s management page.

You can assign a different owner to the record when:

• The status of the record is NOT Closed.
• You have permission to reassign a record.

The application disables the Reassign button when either of the above conditions are not met.

Assigning a record to a different owner is considered manual triage. When you assign a record to a different owner, the application disables the Triage action button.

How to Reassign an Owner

Follow these steps to reassign an owner to a record:

1. Access the record to be updated.
2. Click Reassign in the Quality Actions pane. The Reassign Owner page appears.
3. Select the appropriate Role.
4. Select the appropriate Owner.
5. Enter any relevant comments or notes about the owner reassignment in the Comments field.
6. Click Submit. The application displays a message stating the owner has been changed.
