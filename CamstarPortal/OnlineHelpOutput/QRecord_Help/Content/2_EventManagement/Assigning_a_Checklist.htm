Assigning a Checklist

Use the Assign Checklist action to assign a checklist template to the record. A checklist ensures the user completes required steps when processing the event record. Checklist templates are defined in Modeling.

When Assigning a Checklist

You can assign a checklist when:

• The status of the record is not Pending, In Review, or Closed.
• You have permission to assign a checklist.
• No data has been saved for the currently assigned checklist.

The application disables the Assign Checklist button when any of the above conditions are not met.

Assign Checklist Page Field Definitions

How to Assign a Checklist

Follow these steps to assign a checklist:

1. Access the quality record. The record's management page appears.
2. Click Assign Checklist in the Quality Actions pane. The Assign Checklist page appears.
3. Select a Checklist Template.
4. Enter Comments as necessary.
5. Click Submit. The application displays a message indicating the checklist was assigned and displays the event’s management page.
