Deleting a Saved Event

You can delete events that have been saved, but not those that have been submitted. The Delete button allows you to delete saved events from any page in the generic event recording pageflow.

Note: Submitted events must be closed. Refer to "Managing Records" for information on closing (resolving) event records.

When Deleting a Saved Event

The Delete button is enabled for saved events only. When you delete a saved event, the application removes it from the Concierge.

Deleted events may be displayed during a quality record search, but they will have a status of Deleted. Deleted events cannot be attached to other quality records.

How to Delete a Saved Event

Follow these steps to delete a saved event:

1. Click the Concierge icon to display the Concierge, if necessary.
2. Click the My Pending Items section to view any pending items.
3. Click the pending item. The appropriate page is displayed in the event recording pageflow.
4. Click the Delete button. A warning appears asking you to confirm you want to delete the record.
5. Click OK. The page closes, and the record is deleted and no longer appears in the Concierge.
