Event Recording

The generic event recording feature allows users to record issues against containers or products. Typically, generic events are recorded against materials that have not been entered into the application. For example, an inspector determines a batch of wafers are out-of-spec for thickness during incoming inspection. The inspector would not start or issue these wafers, but could record a generic event for quality tracking purposes.

Generic events will then be reviewed to determine further action. Events can also be closed if it is determined that they are a non-issue that can be resolved without further processing.
