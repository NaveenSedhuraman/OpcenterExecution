Resuming Work on Pending Items

Event records that have been saved, but not submitted, are pending items. You can only access saved records and resume the event recording pageflow from the Concierge. The application adds pending items to the My Pending Items section of the Concierge.

When Resuming Work on Pending Items

Clicking a pending item displays the page of the pageflow that was open when the pageflow was last closed. This may not be the page in the pageflow where you clicked Save. You must navigate to that page. Also, any information on the last page you had entered before canceling the transaction will be lost, but other data entered between clicking Save and Cancel will be saved.

How to Access a Pending Record

Follow these steps to access a pending item:

1. Click the Concierge icon to display the Concierge, if necessary.
2. Click the My Pending Items section to view any pending items.
3. Click the pending item. The appropriate page is displayed in the event recording pageflow.
4. Continue your work on the saved record. You can:
• Save the record again and continue later.
• Finish the pageflow and click the Submit button to complete the record.
• Delete the record by clicking the Delete button.
