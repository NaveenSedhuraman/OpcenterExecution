Viewing Approval Capture History

The application allows you to view the history for current and past approval cycles. The history details include information such as approver roles, names, decisions, and comments captured during the approval process.

When Viewing Approval Capture History

When viewing approval capture history, you select an approval cycle. Note that a cycle starts when the application routes an approval and ends when all users have made an approval decision on the quality record resolution. The cycle also ends when the owner of the record cancels the approval.

You can view the approval cycle history for quality record resolutions on the following pages:

• Sign Approval page, when approving the resolution
• Management page, specifically the Resolution tab

How to View Approval Capture History

Follow these steps to view approval capture history:

1. Access the record’s management page.

Or

The Sign Approval page.

2. Follow these steps according to the page you are viewing:

If you are viewing the . . .

Then . . .

Record's management page
a. Click the Resolution tab. The Resolution tab appears.
b. Select View History in the Approval Sheet Cycle section. The application enables the Approval Cycle field.
c. Select a date from the Approval Cycle field. The application updates the grid with information captured during that approval cycle.
d. Select an approver to view any special instructions or comments entered by that approver.

Sign Approval page
a. Select View History in the Approval Sheet Cycle section. The application enables the Approval Cycle field.
b. Select a date from the Approval Cycle field. The application updates the grid with information captured during that approval cycle.
c. Select an approver to view any special instructions or comments entered by that approver.
