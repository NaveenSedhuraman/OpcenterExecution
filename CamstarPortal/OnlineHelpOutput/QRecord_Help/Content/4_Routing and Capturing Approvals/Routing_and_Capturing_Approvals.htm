Routing and Capturing Approvals

Siemens provides a way to establish and implement formalized approval processes designed to enforce planning and monitoring methodologies, thereby maintaining quality levels throughout production. Each approval process is completely configurable and consists of a series of steps or gates that are validated by pre-defined groups of approvers. The approval process is a configurable method that enables users to request approval for resolution of a quality record.
