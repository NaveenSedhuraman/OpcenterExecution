Approving Quality Record Resolutions

The approval process for a quality record begins when the record owner routes the approval sheet to the first level approvers. The application does the following:

• Creates an approval task in the Concierge of the specified approvers on the approval sheet
• Generates a message and approval action in the Message Center of the specified approvers and appropriate role members
• Generates and sends an e-mail notification to all approvers and appropriate role members (if one has been configured by a system administrator)
Note: The application is able to resolve the entries in its distribution list so that the user defined in the role who is also specified as the approver receives only one e-mail.

Role members can also access the approval page from the Quality Search page and the quality record’s management page using the Approve action.

When Approving a Quality Record Resolution

When approving a quality record resolution, the approval process consists of:

• Viewing the quality record resolution and approval details
• Capturing an approval decision
• Capturing an electronic signature, if applicable

When you select an approval task in the Concierge or the Approve action on the Message Center, Quality Search, or record management page, the application displays the Sign Approval page for the quality record. You can view instructions or comments associated with an approver by selecting that user in the Approvers grid on the Sign Approval page.

Depending on how the approval decision options were defined, the application may require you to enter Approval Comments before submitting your decision. For example, an approval decision list may be created with two decisions: Accepted and Rejected. For the Rejected decision, he selects the Include Comments option. If the approver selects Rejected when approving the quality record resolution, the application updates the Approval Comments field to indicate that comments are required.

When Submitting an Approval Decision

The application displays the ESig pop-up if a valid electronic signature is required to record the approval decision. (Electronic signature requirements for quality records are set at the organization level.) The item is removed from the approver's Concierge and Message Center after the approval decision is submitted.

Sign Approval Page

The Sign Approval page appears when you click an approval task in the Concierge or click the Approve action on one of the following pages:

• Message Center
• Quality Search page
• The record’s management page

How to Approve a Quality Record Resolution

Follow these steps to approve a quality record resolution:

1. Access the Sign Approval page. The Sign Approval page appears.
2. Select your Role. The Approval for list is updated.
3. Select a user ID from the Approval for field.
4. Select an Approval Decision.
5. Enter any comments or notes about the approval decision in the Approval Comments field if required.
6. Click Submit. If the application has been configured to require an electronic signature for your approval, the ESig Popup appears. Otherwise, the application displays a message stating you have completed the approval and displays the record’s management page.
