Canceling Approvals

You can cancel the approval process for a quality record at any time regardless of whether approvers have entered decisions. The Cancel Approval action is available from the quality record’s management page only.

When Canceling an Approval

You can cancel an approval when:

• The status of the record is In Review.
• You are the record owner or have ownership permissions.

The application requires you to enter comments when canceling an approval. The application updates the record management page with approval cancellation information when you click the Submit button on the Approval Cancellation page. You may also see an ESig pop-up before the record management page is updated if the Cancel Approval Sheet service is included in the Electronic Signature Txn Map on the Organization object.

Upon successful cancellation, the application removes the approval message from the following:

• Concierge of the assignee
• Message Center of the assignee
• Message Center of all users in the same role as the assignee (if the Substitute Option is set to Role)

You can view approval cancellation information in the Approval Sheet Cycle history after canceling an approval.

Approval Cancellation Page Button Definitions

This table defines the buttons on the Approval Cancellation page.

Button

Click this button to . . .

Cancel

Display a message asking you to confirm you want to cancel the action. Clicking OK displays the record’s management page.

Submit

Initiate cancellation of the approval process and display the record’s management page. If the cancellation is successful, the application displays a message stating the approval process has been canceled.

How to Cancel an Approval

Follow these steps to cancel an approval:

1. Access the quality record. The record's management page appears.
2. Click Cancel Approval in the Quality Actions pane. The Approval Cancellation page appears.
3. Enter an explanation for the cancellation in the Comments field.
4. Click Submit. The application displays a message stating the approval process has been canceled and displays the record’s management page. Additionally, the approval message is removed from the Concierge of the assignee, from the Message Center of the assignee and, if applicable, all users in the same role as the assignee.
