Assigning Approval Templates

Approval templates contain a list of approval roles, the corresponding approvers, and a list of pre-defined approval decisions. Assigning an approval template to a quality record ensures the application presents the appropriate decisions to the appropriate approvers at the time of approval.

When Assigning Approval Templates

Approval template assignment can occur automatically or manually for quality records. The application assigns approval templates to quality records automatically based on record type. Approval templates are assigned to quality records in the organization's category processing map.

If no approval templates are assigned for quality records at the organization level, the application allows the user resolving the quality record to select and modify an approval template before resolving the record. Refer to the Opcenter EX MDD Modeling User Guide or the Opcenter EX CR Modeling User Guide for information on assigning approval templates when defining category maps.

Note: If you set up category maps for the organization and indicated that approval is required for the quality record type without specifying an approval template, the application requires an operator with ownership rights to assign an approval template at the time of resolution approval.

When an approval template is assigned to a quality record, it becomes an approval sheet. Any changes you make to an approval sheet do not affect the approval template on which it was based.

When Editing Approval Sheets Assigned to Quality Records

You can edit approval sheets assigned to a quality record on the Quality Object Resolution page (during the quality record resolution process). Refer to "How to Edit Approval Information" and "How to Add Approvers for the Quality Resolution Approval" for information on editing approval sheets at quality record resolution.
