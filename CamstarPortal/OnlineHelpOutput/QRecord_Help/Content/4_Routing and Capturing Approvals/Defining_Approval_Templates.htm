Defining Approval Templates

You must define or modify these modeling objects to define and use approval templates:

• Approval Decision List
• Approval Template (Routing Sheet)
• Organization

Because the application requires you to associate an approval decision list with an approval template, you must define an approval decision list first. Approval decision lists contain the decisions that will be presented to designated approvers when the application requires approval for quality record resolution. For example, an approval decision list may contain the decisions: Accepted and Rejected.

Define an approval template next. Approval templates identify the users who need to approve quality record resolutions. Additionally, the approval template specifies a list of possible decisions (the associated approval decision list) for those users.

If you want the application to assign approval templates to quality records automatically (for quality record resolution), you must assign approval templates to the Organization modeling object. When you assign approval templates to an organization, the application assigns the appropriate approval template to a quality record automatically based on the record’s category type and resolution action.

Note: If you do not assign any approval templates to an organization, an approval template can be assigned when resolving the quality record.

Refer to the Opcenter EX MDD Modeling User Guide or the Opcenter EX CR Modeling User Guide for information on defining or modifying these modeling objects.
