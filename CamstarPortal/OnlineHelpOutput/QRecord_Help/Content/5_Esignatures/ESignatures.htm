Electronic Signatures

The Electronic Signatures (ESignature) module is designed to establish validation of quality measures and approvals by responsible members of an organization. In this regard, it complies with the requirements stipulated by the FDA 21 CFR Part 11 regulation.

Note: The ESignature module is available in the Opcenter EX MDD and Opcenter EX CR bundles.

The application includes the ability to configure electronic signature requirements for the completion of selected quality (event) record transactions and approvals. This feature is implemented by defining an electronic signature transaction map in the Organization modeling object. Depending on how it is configured, you are asked to enter a user name and password or a password only to complete the requirement.
