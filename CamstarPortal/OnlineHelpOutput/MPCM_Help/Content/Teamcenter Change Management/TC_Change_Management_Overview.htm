Teamcenter Change Management

The functionality described in this section is available only when your organization has integrated the Siemen's Teamcenter application with the Camstar Enterprise Platform (CEP). Refer to the Camstar Teamcenter Integration Guide for information.

Integrating the Siemen's Teamcenter application with the CEP enables you to download Teamcenter engineering change notices (ECNs) as a change management packages. You can then perform change management tasks on the packages in CEP. Siemens provides additional modeling objects and CEP functionality to process change packages downloaded from Teamcenter.
