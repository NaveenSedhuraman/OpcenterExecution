Routing and Capturing Approvals

Opcenter Execution Medical Device and Diagnostics and Opcenter Execution Core provide a way to establish and implement formalized change management approval processes designed to enforce planning and monitoring methodologies, thereby maintaining quality levels. Each approval process is completely configurable and consists of a series of steps or gates that are validated by predefined groups of approvers. The approval process is a configurable method that enables users to request approval for a change management package.

You must have roles with appropriate permissions assigned to use each of these options.

Note: The approval process for Teamcenter change management packages varies from the default Camstar Change processes. Refer to the "Teamcenter Change Management" appendix for information.
